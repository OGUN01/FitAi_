## Health Connect Disclosure Draft

This section is designed to be inserted into the FitAI Privacy Policy to comply with Google Play Health Connect requirements.

---

```html
<h2>10. Google Health Connect</h2>
<p>FitAI integrates with Google Health Connect to provide a comprehensive view of your fitness journey and to deliver personalized, AI-powered workout recommendations. This integration allows us to sync health and fitness data between FitAI and other apps on your device.</p>

<h3>a. Data We Read from Health Connect</h3>
<p>With your explicit permission, FitAI reads the following types of data from Health Connect:</p>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Activity Data:</strong> Steps, distance, and active minutes to assess your daily activity levels.</li>
    <li><strong>Vitals:</strong> Heart rate data to understand your cardiovascular health and intensity during workouts.</li>
    <li><strong>Sleep:</strong> Sleep duration and stages to optimize your recovery and workout scheduling.</li>
    <li><strong>Workouts:</strong> Historical workout and exercise sessions to build a complete picture of your fitness history.</li>
    <li><strong>Calories:</strong> Total and active calories burned to align your nutrition and exercise plans.</li>
    <li><strong>Body Measurements:</strong> Weight and body fat percentage to track your physical progress over time.</li>
</ul>

<h3>b. Data We Write to Health Connect</h3>
<p>FitAI can contribute your workout data to Health Connect, making it available to other health and fitness apps you use. We write:</p>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Exercise Sessions:</strong> Details of workouts you complete within FitAI, including duration and type.</li>
    <li><strong>Active Calories Burned:</strong> The energy expenditure calculated for your FitAI workouts.</li>
</ul>

<h3>c. Purpose and Use of Data</h3>
<p>We use Health Connect data exclusively to provide and improve the FitAI experience. Specifically:</p>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Personalized AI Recommendations:</strong> Our AI (Google Gemini) analyzes your health metrics to suggest workouts that match your current fitness level and recovery state.</li>
    <li><strong>Progress Monitoring:</strong> We provide detailed analytics and visualizations of your fitness trends.</li>
    <li><strong>Nutrition Alignment:</strong> We help you understand your caloric needs based on your actual activity levels.</li>
</ul>

<h3>d. Data Protection and Limited Use</h3>
<p>Your health data is sensitive, and we treat it with the highest level of security:</p>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Local Processing:</strong> Much of your health data is processed locally on your device.</li>
    <li><strong>Secure Storage:</strong> Aggregated data used for AI analysis is stored securely in our encrypted Supabase database.</li>
    <li><strong>No Data Selling:</strong> We never sell your Health Connect data to third parties.</li>
    <li><strong>No Advertising:</strong> We do not use your Health Connect data for advertising purposes.</li>
    <li><strong>No Credit Scoring:</strong> We do not use this data for creditworthiness, lending, or insurance underwriting.</li>
</ul>

<h3>e. User Control and Rights</h3>
<p>You have full control over your health data:</p>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Revoke Access:</strong> You can change or revoke FitAI's access to Health Connect at any time through the FitAI app settings or the Android system Health Connect settings.</li>
    <li><strong>Data Deletion:</strong> You can request the deletion of all health data synced to FitAI by contacting us or using the "Delete Account" feature in the app.</li>
    <li><strong>Access and Export:</strong> You have the right to access and export your data as per GDPR and other applicable privacy laws.</li>
</ul>

<h3>f. Data Retention</h3>
<p>We retain data retrieved from Health Connect only as long as it is necessary to provide our services or until you request its deletion. If you disconnect Health Connect or delete your account, we will cease accessing new data and delete existing data according to our standard retention policy.</li>
```
