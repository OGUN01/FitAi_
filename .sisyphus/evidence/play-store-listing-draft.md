- **Guided Sessions:** Step-by-step instructions with visual guides for every exercise.

- **7-Day Meal Plans:** Personalized weekly nutrition strategies to fuel your performance.
- **Macro Tracking:** Monitor your protein, carb, and fat intake with ease.

- **Hydration Tracker:** Stay on top of your water intake with smart reminders.

- **Interactive Progress Charts:** Visualize your weight, body fat, and muscle mass trends over time.
